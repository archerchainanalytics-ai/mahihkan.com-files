On the Record — What This Is

FormDepot™ is not a template generator. It does not produce documents on demand using a language model. It does not autocomplete your grant proposal or synthesize a framework from a prompt. Every template in FormDepot™ is a professionally authored, legally reviewed, field-tested document — structured by a human who has used it in a real context, processed through the SalvageBotX™ productization pipeline, and delivered via RaPaX™ as a ready-to-use file. You are buying a finished product, not a draft from a machine that has never filed a grant application in its life.

Origin

Why This Exists

Professional document templates are expensive to produce and difficult to find at production quality. Grant proposals, compliance frameworks, business registration packages, legal notices, audit checklists — the kind of documents that actually get accepted, filed, and approved — require domain knowledge that most template marketplaces either lack or charge for through a subscription that gives you access to a library of mediocre approximations.

FormDepot™ was built on a different premise. SalvageBotX™ identifies high-value document structures from real professional codebases and operational workflows. Those structures are refined, authored to production standard, and listed as standalone purchasable templates. The pipeline from identification to delivery is automated. The authorship is not. Every template shipped through FormDepot™ has a human signature behind it.
